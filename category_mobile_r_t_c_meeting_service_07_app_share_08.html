◆ appShareWithReplayKit()

- (void) appShareWithReplayKit

This method is used to share content with ReplayKit. This method will take effect in iOS 11 or new.

Warning
After this interface has been called, App UI content should update after about 0.25 second for ReplayKit cannot send out if UI content does not change.

Extends class MobileRTCMeetingService.
